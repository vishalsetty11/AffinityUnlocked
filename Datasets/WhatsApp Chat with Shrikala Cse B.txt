3. **Importance of Geographic Data**: Geographic data plays a crucial role in understanding M2M traffic patterns in smart cities. Electric Toothbrush with Revolvable Brush Head

Field of Invention:
The present invention pertains to electric toothbrush technology and specifically focuses on a novel design involving a brush head capable of undergoing movement along a path of revolution, maintaining a consistent brushing plane orientation relative to the teeth.

Abstract:
This invention introduces an electric toothbrush featuring a handle, a brush head defining a brushing plane, and a guide arrangement facilitating the movement of the brush head along an endless path of revolution. The axis of this revolution aligns substantially parallel to the brushing plane, ensuring consistent orientation relative to the teeth during brushing. Traditional electric toothbrushes often employ rotational or oscillatory motions, resulting in uneven cleaning efficacy due to differences in bristle movement. By enabling a revolvable brush head, this invention addresses these shortcomings, providing more uniform cleaning while optimizing energy efficiency. The innovation presented here aims to enhance the effectiveness and usability of electric toothbrushes.

Objects of Invention:
1. To provide an electric toothbrush with a brush head capable of movement along a path of revolution, maintaining a consistent brushing plane orientation relative to the teeth.
2. To overcome the uneven cleaning efficacy associated with traditional electric toothbrushes by enabling a revolvable brush head.
3. To optimize energy efficiency by reducing the energy consumption associated with oscillatory motions commonly found in prior electric toothbrush designs.
4. To enhance the removal of particles from the teeth surface by enabling a continuous, even movement of the brush head, minimizing particle buildup in the bristles.
5. To innovate within the field of electric toothbrush technology, addressing existing shortcomings without introducing new problems, thereby improving overall brushing experience and oral hygiene.

**Summary of the Invention:**

The present invention introduces an electric toothbrush featuring a handle, a brush head defining a brushing plane, and a guide arrangement supported by the handle. This guide arrangement supports the brush head to undergo movement along an endless path of revolution with an axis of revolution parallel to the brushing plane. This arrangement ensures that the brushing plane maintains its orientation relative to the surface of teeth throughout the movement, thus enhancing efficiency and improving brushing results. The brush head's revolving motion allows even wear of bristles and gentle brushing on gum tissue. The invention offers enhanced energy efficiency, and a continuous, linear brushing movement.
